Online Safety & Bullying Prevention

Ensuring your safety online is crucial, especially when seeking emotional support. Here are some key tips to stay safe and avoid cyberbullying:

  • Never share personal information (real name, address, phone number) with strangers online.
  • Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication on your accounts.
  • Be cautious when talking to new people onlineâ€”verify their identities before trusting them.
  • Report and block any users who harass or bully you on social media or support platforms.
  • If you experience online bullying, reach out to a trusted friend, family member, or counselor.
  • If your life is in danger, call or text 112 or 911 immediately.
